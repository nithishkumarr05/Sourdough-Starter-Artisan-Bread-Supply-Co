Our Story

Crafting Tradition
One Loaf at a Time

Founded in 2019 with a mission to preserve the art of natural fermentation and connect bakers with heritage grains and living cultures.

Our Philosophy

Slow Bread. Real Flavor.

We believe that great bread starts with living ingredients. That's why we nurture heirloom starters, source organic grains directly from regenerative farms, and mill our flours fresh for every batch.

No shortcuts. No preservatives. Just the honest, time-honored craft of sourdough — shared with bakers who care as much about the process as the result.

100% Organic • Stone Ground • Wild Fermented
Core Values

What Guides Us

Heritage Preservation

We protect and share heirloom starter cultures — some over 100 years old — keeping traditional bread-making alive.

Regenerative Farming

We partner with farms that rebuild soil health, sequester carbon, and grow nutrient-dense grains.

Education First

Free resources, workshops, and a supportive community — because every baker deserves to succeed.
