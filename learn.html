Solutions

Common Baking Issues Solved

Why is my starter not bubbling?

Your starter may need warmer temperatures (75-80°F) or more frequent feedings. Try using filtered water and organic rye flour.

My bread came out dense and gummy

This is often under-fermentation. Extend your bulk fermentation time and check your dough temperature.

How do I get a more open crumb?

Increase hydration, handle dough gently during shaping, and ensure proper fermentation.

My scoring didn't open properly

Score at a 45-degree angle with a sharp lame. Make sure your dough has enough surface tension.
