Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Orders ship within 2-3 business days. Standard shipping takes 3-5 days.

Do you offer wholesale pricing?

Yes! Contact our wholesale team at wholesale@sourdoughlab.com

My starter arrived inactive — what do I do?

Contact us within 48 hours with photos — we'll send a replacement immediately.

Can I visit the bakery lab?

Our lab is not open for walk-ins, but we offer scheduled pickup appointments.
